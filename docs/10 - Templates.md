# 10 - Templates

This section contains the canonical Trail Framework templates referenced in the v1.0 docs.

Templates are organized by their location in the scaffold:

- **[Intent Package](templates/intent-package/)** — the three files every Architect produces to define a unit of work
- **[Meta Package](templates/meta-package/)** — the files that live in `trail/meta/` to anchor project identity and policy

Do not modify these reference templates directly. Copy them into your intent or meta folder and edit from there.
